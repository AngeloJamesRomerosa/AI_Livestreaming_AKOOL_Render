0. CLIENT USER GUIDE (START HERE)
===============================================================================

This section is written for the end user -- no technical knowledge required.
Follow these steps every time you want to go live with AI face swap.


WHAT YOU NEED BEFORE YOU START
--------------------------------
  - The app URL (e.g. https://your-app.onrender.com)
  - Your AKOOL Client ID and Client Secret (or AKOOL API Key)
  - A clear photo of the face you want to appear on stream (JPG or PNG,
    one face visible, good lighting, facing forward)
  - A modern browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)
  - OBS Studio installed (if using OBS output), OR your RTMP stream key
    from your streaming platform (if going live directly)

NOTE: This version runs entirely in your browser. No software installation
is needed beyond a browser. All AI processing happens in the cloud via AKOOL.


WHAT THE SCREEN LOOKS LIKE
----------------------------
When you open the app, you will see:

  LEFT SIDE -- Setup panels (accordion menus you open one by one):
    [1] AI Model         -- which AI engine is active (AKOOL / Agora)
    [2] Authentication   -- where you enter your AKOOL credentials
    [3] Source Face      -- where you upload the target face photo
    [4] Session          -- stream quality, background, and start/stop controls

  RIGHT SIDE -- Live view area:
    - Your Camera        -- your real webcam feed
    - AI Faceswap Output -- the face-swapped result (appears after session starts)
    - Virtual Camera Output panel -- tabs for OBS URL, Pop Out, Go Live
    - Activity Log       -- messages and status shown here
    - Performance Metrics -- live fps and latency numbers


STEP-BY-STEP: HOW TO USE THE APP EVERY SESSION
------------------------------------------------

  STEP 1 -- Open the App
  -----------------------
  Open Chrome or Edge and go to the app URL.
  Example: https://your-app.onrender.com

  Allow camera access when the browser asks.
  The left side shows setup panels. The right side shows your camera once
  the session starts.


  STEP 2 -- Authenticate
  -----------------------
  1. Click the "Authentication" panel to open it.
  2. Choose your login method:
       - "Credentials" tab: enter your Client ID and Client Secret
       - "API Key" tab: enter your API Key directly
  3. Click the "Authenticate" button.
  4. The status indicator turns GREEN when successful.

  If it fails: double-check your credentials and try again.
  Error code 1101 means the token expired -- click Authenticate again.
  Error code 1104 means your AKOOL quota is used up -- contact your provider.


  STEP 3 -- Set Your Source Face
  --------------------------------
  1. Click the "Source Face Image" panel to open it.
  2. Option A -- Upload from your computer: click "Upload" and select a photo.
     Option B -- Paste an image URL: enter a public image URL in the text box.
  3. Click "Detect Faces" to confirm the face is found.
  4. A small preview of the detected face appears when successful.

  Tips for best results:
    - Use a clear, well-lit frontal photo
    - Only one face should be visible
    - JPG or PNG format, minimum 300x300 px
    - No sunglasses, masks, or heavy shadows


  STEP 4 -- Configure Stream Settings (inside Session panel)
  -----------------------------------------------------------
  1. Click the "Session" panel to open it.
  2. Under "Stream Quality", choose your resolution and frame rate:
       Auto (640x360)   -- lightest on your browser, good for testing
       480p             -- balanced quality
       720p             -- good quality (recommended)
       1080p            -- highest quality, heavier on browser
  3. Choose your frame rate: 15 / 25 / 30 fps
  4. Background Removal (optional -- expand "Background Removal" section):
       - Select a preset: Blur, Black, Classroom, Gym, White Room,
         Pink Wall, Beach, or upload your own image
       - Click "Preview Camera (BG Test)" to see the effect before starting
       - Background Change: Output = applied to what OBS receives
         Background Change: Input = applied to your camera before AKOOL


  STEP 5 -- Start the Session
  -----------------------------
  1. Click "Start Faceswap".
  2. The app takes 5-15 seconds to connect to AKOOL and join the channel.
  3. Once ready, the AI Faceswap Output panel shows your face-swapped video.
  4. The Activity Log shows "Live faceswap is active!" when ready.
  5. The Virtual Camera Output panel appears -- connect this to your platform.


  STEP 6 -- CONNECT TO YOUR STREAMING PLATFORM
  ----------------------------------------------
  Choose ONE method:

  --- Option A: Using OBS (Recommended) ---

    1. In the Virtual Camera Output panel, click "Copy" to copy the viewer URL.
    2. Open OBS Studio.
    3. In OBS: Sources --> + --> Browser Source --> paste the URL.
    4. Set Width/Height to match your quality preset:
         720p  --> Width: 1280  Height: 720
         1080p --> Width: 1920  Height: 1080
    5. Click OK. The AI face-swapped video appears in OBS.
    6. OBS Settings --> Stream:
         Select your platform (YouTube, Twitch, etc.)
         Enter your Stream Key
    7. Click "Start Streaming" in OBS.

    IMPORTANT: Copy the OBS URL fresh at the start of every session.
    The URL contains a one-time key that expires when the session ends.

    Where to find your stream key:
      YouTube  : YouTube Studio --> Go Live --> Stream Key
      Twitch   : Creator Dashboard --> Settings --> Stream --> Primary Stream Key
      Stripchat: Performer Dashboard --> Broadcast Settings --> Stream Key

  --- Option B: Pop Out Window ---

    1. Click "Open Pop Out Window" in the Virtual Camera Output panel.
    2. A borderless window opens showing the AI stream.
    3. In OBS: Sources --> + --> Window Capture --> select the popup window.
    4. Configure OBS stream settings and start streaming.

  --- Option C: Go Live Directly (No OBS) ---

    1. Get your RTMP URL + stream key from your platform dashboard.
    2. In the Virtual Camera Output panel, click the "Go Live" tab.
    3. Paste the full RTMP URL (server + key combined).
       Example: rtmp://a.rtmp.youtube.com/live2/YOUR-STREAM-KEY
    4. Click "Go Live".
    5. Check your platform dashboard -- stream appears live within 10-30 seconds.


  STEP 7 -- While You Are Live
  ------------------------------
  - Keep the app browser tab OPEN AND VISIBLE while streaming.
    This is important -- if you minimize or hide the tab, the frame relay
    to OBS may slow down. Use a second monitor if possible.
  - Watch the Activity Log for warnings or errors.
  - Performance Metrics show FPS and latency.
  - If the AI output looks frozen: stop and restart the session.


  STEP 8 -- End the Session
  --------------------------
  1. Stop your stream on the platform first (OBS: "Stop Streaming").
  2. Click "Stop Session" in the app.
  3. The session ends and the AI output panel goes blank.
  4. The OBS URL from this session is now expired -- do not reuse it.


QUICK CHECKLIST BEFORE EVERY STREAM
--------------------------------------
  [ ] App is open in Chrome or Edge
  [ ] Authenticated (status indicator is green)
  [ ] Source face is uploaded and detected
  [ ] Stream quality preset is selected
  [ ] Session is started (AI Faceswap Output shows video)
  [ ] OBS URL is freshly copied from the Virtual Camera Output panel
  [ ] App tab is visible on screen (not minimized)
  [ ] Streaming platform dashboard confirms stream is live
  [ ] Activity Log shows no errors

1. WHAT THE CLIENT EXPECTS
===============================================================================

CORE REQUIREMENT
----------------
Go live on a streaming platform (YouTube, Twitch, Stripchat, etc.) with a
real camera feed replaced by an AI face-swapped version in real time.


WHAT THE AI DOES
----------------
  - The client's face is replaced with a chosen target face via AKOOL's cloud AI.
  - The background can optionally be removed or replaced (blur, solid color,
    or a virtual background image).
  - The output is a clean video stream that goes to OBS or directly to a platform.


WHAT THE CLIENT CONTROLS
--------------------------
  Authentication     - Enter AKOOL credentials to activate the AI
  Source Face        - Upload a photo of the face to swap onto
  Stream Quality     - Resolution, FPS, and bitrate preset
  Background         - Preset or custom image; applied to camera input or output
  Output Method      - OBS Browser Source, Pop Out Window, or direct RTMP


WHAT THE CLIENT SEES AS OUTPUT
-------------------------------
  [Webcam] --> [AKOOL AI Face Swap] --> [Output]
                                         |-- OBS Browser Source (recommended)
                                         |-- Pop Out Window    (OBS alternative)
                                         +-- Go Live / RTMP    (direct, no OBS)

2. LIVE STREAMING PLATFORM INTEGRATION
===============================================================================

METHOD A -- OBS BROWSER SOURCE (RECOMMENDED)
---------------------------------------------
Best for: Streamers already using OBS Studio

Setup:
  1. Start a faceswap session in the app.
  2. Copy the viewer URL from the Virtual Camera Output panel.
  3. OBS Studio:
       Sources --> + --> Browser Source --> paste URL
       Width/Height: match your quality preset (720p = 1280x720)
       Check: Shutdown source when not visible
       Check: Refresh browser when scene becomes active
       Click OK
  4. OBS Settings --> Stream:
       Service: YouTube / Twitch / Custom RTMP
       Server: your platform's RTMP ingest URL
       Stream Key: your stream key
       Apply --> OK
  5. Click "Start Streaming"

  Where to find stream keys:
    YouTube  : YouTube Studio --> Go Live
               RTMP Server: rtmp://a.rtmp.youtube.com/live2
    Twitch   : Creator Dashboard --> Settings --> Stream
               RTMP Server: rtmp://live.twitch.tv/app
    Stripchat: Performer Dashboard --> Broadcast Settings

  Notes:
    - Always copy the OBS URL fresh at the start of each session
    - If Browser Source goes black: right-click --> Refresh in OBS
    - Keep the app tab visible on screen while streaming


-----------------------------------------------------------------------

METHOD B -- OBS WINDOW CAPTURE (POP OUT WINDOW)
------------------------------------------------
Best for: When Browser Source has issues, or as an alternative

Setup:
  1. Start a session --> click "Open Pop Out Window"
  2. A borderless popup appears showing the AI stream
  3. OBS: Sources --> + --> Window Capture --> select the popup
  4. Configure OBS stream settings and go live (same as Method A, Steps 4-5)


-----------------------------------------------------------------------

METHOD C -- DIRECT RTMP (GO LIVE -- NO OBS REQUIRED)
------------------------------------------------------
Best for: Streaming directly to a platform without OBS

Setup:
  1. Get your RTMP URL and Stream Key from your platform:

       Platform       RTMP Server URL                           Key Location
       -------------  ----------------------------------------  ---------------------------
       YouTube Live   rtmp://a.rtmp.youtube.com/live2           YouTube Studio --> Go Live
       Twitch         rtmp://live.twitch.tv/app                 Creator Dashboard --> Stream
       Stripchat      (shown in performer dashboard)            Performer Dashboard
       Facebook Live  rtmps://live-api-s.facebook.com:443/rtmp  Facebook Live Producer

  2. Combine: RTMP Server URL + / + Stream Key
     Example: rtmp://a.rtmp.youtube.com/live2/xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx

  3. Start a session --> click the "Go Live" tab in the Output panel
  4. Paste the full RTMP URL
  5. Click "Go Live"
  6. Check your platform dashboard -- stream appears within 10-30 seconds

4. KNOWN LIMITATIONS
===============================================================================

KEEP THE BROWSER TAB VISIBLE
------------------------------
What happens:
  This version runs all frame processing in your browser (not on a server).
  Chrome throttles background tabs -- if you minimize or switch away from the
  app tab, the frame relay to OBS may slow down or stutter.

What to do:
  Keep the app tab open and visible while streaming. A second monitor works well.
  The app uses a background keep-alive to reduce (but not eliminate) throttling.


AI OUTPUT DELAY INCREASES OVER TIME
--------------------------------------
What happens:
  The AI output may show a gradually increasing delay over the course of a
  session -- starting around 300ms and possibly reaching 1 second or more
  after 30-60 minutes.

Why it happens:
  AKOOL's AI cloud delivers processed frames in bursts. This is a limitation
  on AKOOL's side and cannot be fully eliminated.

What to do:
  The stream stays continuous -- viewers will not notice since audio and video
  are delayed equally. If delay feels too high, stop and restart the session.
  Restarting every 45-60 minutes is recommended for long streams.


OBS URL CHANGES EVERY SESSION
--------------------------------
What happens:
  The URL for OBS Browser Source contains a one-time session key that expires
  when the session ends. Reusing an old URL shows a black screen in OBS.

What to do:
  After starting a new session, always copy the URL fresh from the Output panel.


NO VIRTUAL CAMERA (BUILT-IN WEBCAM)
-------------------------------------
The virtual webcam feature (for Zoom, Discord, Teams) is not available in
this version. Use OBS Browser Source or Go Live (RTMP) instead.

5. TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK REFERENCE
===============================================================================

  Symptom                           Likely Cause                 What To Do
  --------------------------------  ---------------------------  ----------------------------------
  OBS Browser Source black screen   Session URL expired          Copy URL fresh; right-click
                                                                 Refresh in OBS
  AI stream stutters or drops fps   Browser tab minimized        Keep app tab visible on screen
  Background shows as black         Preset not matching          Select a preset and wait for
                                                                 model to load (first use only)
  Session error 1101                Login token expired          Click Authenticate again
  Session error 1104                AKOOL quota used up          Contact your provider
  Face not detected                 Photo quality issue          Use a clearer frontal photo
  OBS relay keeps disconnecting     Rare server issue            Stop and restart the session
  AI output delay increasing        Normal after 30-60 min       Stop and restart the session
  Video quality looks poor          Resolution preset too low    Try 720p or 1080p preset
  Background preview not working    Camera permission            Allow camera access in browser
